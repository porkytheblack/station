Core

Cron & triggers

Standard cron expressions, interval-based scheduling, and event-driven triggers. Write it once in your codebase. Signal handles the rest.

Execution history

Every run logged. Every failure captured. Full replay capability. You weren't watching — but the logbook was.

Reliability

Automatic retries

Configurable retry policies with exponential backoff. Failed jobs don't disappear — they get back in line and try again.

Idempotency keys

Safe by default. Every job gets an idempotency key. Duplicate dispatches don't create duplicate runs.

Scale

Concurrency control

Set max concurrent executions per job type. Prevent thundering herds. Define exactly how much load your services absorb.

Job chaining

Define dependent workflows where one job's output triggers the next. Complex pipelines without complex infrastructure.
